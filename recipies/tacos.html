Tacos

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • tortillas
  • shredded cheese
  • taco seasoning
  • diced tomatoes
  • shredded lettuce

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet, add some taco seasoning and a bit of water

  2. Drain juices and fat grom the skillet

  3. Add beef, cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes to tortilla

  4. Serve fresh and hot
